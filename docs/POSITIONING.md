# Public Positioning

Preferred short descriptions for Windowstead:

- A desktop-resident idle colony sim that lives at the edge of your screen.
- An ambient colony companion where workers keep building while you use your desktop.
- A glanceable edge-docked settlement sim for low-attention play.
- A tiny autonomous colony that runs as a persistent desktop companion.

Use product-first wording in public copy:

- edge-docked desktop companion
- ambient, glanceable, low-interruption play
- player plans and places; workers execute
- bottom dock and side dock as distinct orientation families

Avoid positioning-led phrasing such as `X meets Y`, `like <named game>`, or `basically <named game> but...` unless there is a specific legal/product reason to mention another title.
